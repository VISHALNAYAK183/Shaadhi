Sirigere Bride and Groom Connect App: Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Policy Statement

At Sirigere Bride and Groom Connect, we are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful platform for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any such behavior or content is strictly prohibited and will be dealt with promptly, including reporting to relevant authorities.

Preventative Measures

  1. Strict Age Verification:
    • All users must be 18 years or older to create an account.
    • Robust age verification processes are in place to ensure compliance.
  2. Content Moderation:
    • All user-generated content is monitored using advanced AI tools to detect inappropriate material.
    • Our moderation team reviews flagged content and takes immediate action when necessary.
  3. Prohibited Content:
    • Any content that exploits or endangers children is strictly forbidden.
    • Users found violating this policy will face account suspension and reporting to law enforcement.
